Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 16, 2026

Run Visuals ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Run Visuals mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the App.

Information We Handle

Running Activity Data

When you connect a third-party fitness service or enter data manually, the following running activity information passes through our servers or is stored on your device:

  • Distance, duration, pace, and split times
  • GPS route data (polylines)
  • Elevation and heart rate data
  • Activity date and type

Third-Party Service Accounts

If you choose to connect your Garmin or Strava account:

  • Garmin Connect: Garmin sends running activity data to our servers via its webhook system. This data is temporarily stored on our servers until the App fetches it to your device.
  • Strava: Running activity data is fetched from the Strava API. Activity summaries are temporarily stored on our servers until fetched by the App; detailed activity data is cached locally on your device.

We only access running activities. We do not access other workout types, social data, or profile information beyond what is needed to identify your activities.

Photos and Camera

If you choose to add a background image to your run card, the App may request access to your device's camera or photo library. Photos you select are used locally to generate your card image. We do not upload or store your photos on our servers.

Analytics Data

We use Google Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage data, including:

  • Screen views
  • Feature usage (which card/sticker designs are used, data sources selected)
  • Card downloads and shares

This data is aggregated and anonymous. It does not include your running activity data, personal details, or photos. Firebase Analytics may collect device identifiers and IP addresses as described in Google's Firebase Privacy documentation.

How We Use Your Information

  • To generate visual run cards and stickers from your activity data
  • To temporarily store your running activities on our servers until they are fetched by the App on your device
  • To improve the App based on anonymous, aggregated usage patterns

Data Storage and Security

  • On your device: Activity data is cached locally using on-device storage.
  • On our servers: Activity data from Garmin and Strava is temporarily stored on our servers (Amazon DynamoDB, hosted in AWS US East) until it is fetched by the App on your device. Once the data is successfully fetched to your device, it is immediately deleted from our servers. Any activity data that is not fetched by the App will be automatically deleted from our servers after 30 days. OAuth tokens for connected services are stored securely on our servers.
  • We use HTTPS for all communication between the App and our servers.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties, except:

  • Service providers: We use AWS for server hosting and database storage, and Google Firebase for analytics.
  • Legal requirements: We may disclose data if required by law or to protect our rights.

Your Choices

  • Disconnect accounts: You can disconnect your Garmin or Strava account at any time through the respective service's settings.
  • Camera and photo access: You can deny or revoke camera/photo permissions in your device settings. The App will still function without these permissions.
  • Delete your data: To request deletion of your data from our servers, contact us at the email below.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy within the App or on our website. Your continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
